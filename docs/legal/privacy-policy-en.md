## 1. One-minute summary

- SaleNoti tracks Shopee Vietnam product prices on your behalf. When you register, we store your email + the products you track + your alert configuration. We send email/push/Telegram alerts when prices drop per your triggers.
- We are an affiliate publisher of the Shopee Affiliate Open API. When you click a deeplink in an alert or on a public page, we earn 1.5–5% commission (varies by category). You pay no extra.
- We DO NOT: auto-apply coupons, override other publishers' / KOCs' affiliate cookies, hide better deals to chase higher commissions, sell your personal data.
- You have rights to: access, correct, erase, restrict processing, object, port your data (Articles 14–22 of Decree 13).
- Contact the DPO any time: legal@salenoti.vn

## 2. Data we collect

### 2.1 At sign-up

- **Email address** — required; used for sign-in + alert delivery.
- **Display name** — only if you sign in via Google OAuth (taken from Google profile `name`).
- **OAuth provider ID** — to link future sign-ins.

### 2.2 As you use the service

- **Tracked products** — the shopee.vn URLs you paste, alert config (trigger type, % threshold, target price).
- **Notification history** — alerts sent, opened, clicked. Retained 365 days.
- **IP address, User-Agent** — for abuse prevention. IP is truncated to `/24` (IPv4) or `/64` (IPv6) in analytics.

### 2.3 If you install the Chrome extension

- **disclosureAcknowledgedAt** — local timestamp confirming you read the disclosure; stored in `chrome.storage` only.
- The extension does NOT read cookies, does NOT override other publishers' affiliate cookies, does NOT inject scripts into non-Shopee pages.

### 2.4 If you opt in to Web Push

- **Push subscription endpoint + keys** — generated by your browser; we store these to deliver push notifications.

### 2.5 If you opt in to the Telegram bot (Phase 2)

- **Telegram chat ID** — when you message `/start <token>` to our bot. You can unbind via `/unsubscribe`.

### 2.6 If you upgrade to Pro / Pro+ (Phase 2)

- **Stripe / VNPay / MoMo customer ID** — we do NOT store card numbers; cards flow directly to the gateway via redirect/iframe.

### 2.7 What we DO NOT collect

Home address, date of birth, government ID, personal tax ID, health information, biometrics, GPS location, browsing history outside Shopee product pages, contacts, Google Drive / Calendar / Photos.

## 6. Security

### 6.1 Technical measures

- TLS 1.2+ everywhere (Vercel + Railway + Atlas enforce by default).
- AES-256 encryption-at-rest (Atlas + Neon defaults).
- Auth.js v5 with JWT rotation + reuse-detection (FR-AUTH-003).
- All secrets managed via Doppler; no `.env` committed.
- Rate limits on every endpoint.

### 6.2 Organizational measures

- Dedicated DPO (Stephen Cheng — Founder).
- 72-hour A05 breach-notification process (template at `A05-breach-notification-template.md`).
- Quarterly Transparency Report (https://salenoti.vn/transparency).
- Annual PDPL training internally.

### 6.3 In case of incident

If we detect a personal-data breach, we will:

1. Notify A05 within 72 hours.
2. Notify you (email) if the incident poses "high risk" per Article 33.
3. Remediate + update TOMs within 7 days.

## 7. Cookies & tracking

We use the following cookies:

- **`authjs.session-token`** — sign-in session (15 minutes), HTTP-only Secure.
- **`authjs.refresh-token`** — refresh token (30 days), HTTP-only Secure, Path-scoped to `/api/auth/refresh`.
- **`salenoti.pre_click_v1`** — marks that you have seen the affiliate-disclosure interstitial (30 days), reduces friction within the same session.
- **`salenoti.ref`** — referral cookie (30 days) if you arrived via an invitation link.

We DO NOT use third-party advertising tracking cookies.

## 8. Minors

SaleNoti is not directed at individuals under 18. If you are under 18, please do not register. If we learn that an account belongs to a minor, we will delete it within 24 hours.
