## 3. Necessity & Proportionality

### 3.1 Lawful basis matrix

- **Consent (Art. 17):** sign-up, push opt-in, Telegram opt-in, marketing email.
- **Contract performance (Art. 17):** Pro subscription billing, alert dispatch, account management.
- **Legitimate interest (Art. 17):** abuse prevention (rate limits), security logging, breach detection.

### 3.2 Data minimisation

- We DO NOT collect: home address (work address is fine), date of birth, government ID, marital status, race, religion, political opinion, health data, biometrics, location, browsing history outside shopee.vn product pages.
- We DO NOT request: Drive / Calendar / Contacts scopes from Google OAuth — only `openid email profile`.
- Browser extension limited to `*://*.shopee.vn/*` only — no `<all_urls>`.

### 3.3 Purpose limitation

Each data category is used ONLY for the stated purpose in §2.2. Function creep prevention:

- Email NOT used for: marketing without separate opt-in, sharing with third parties, profile enrichment.
- Watchlist activity NOT used for: psychological profiling, behavioral nudging beyond alert UX.

## 4. Risks to data subjects

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|:-:|:-:|---|
| Account takeover via leaked credential | Medium | High | Magic-link single-use 15-min · JWT refresh rotation with reuse-detection (FR-AUTH-003) · WebAuthn (Phase 3) |
| Data breach exposing tracked products | Low | Medium | MongoDB Atlas encryption-at-rest + TLS in transit · RBAC on read paths · 365-day audit log |
| Function creep (using watchlist data for behavioral profiling) | Low | High | Internal policy + Privacy Policy commitment · Quarterly Transparency Report (FR-LEGAL-002 §1 #7) |
| Cross-border transfer to US-hosted SaaS | Medium | Medium | See `cross-border-transfer-impact-assessment.md` · all recipients have SOC 2 or ISO 27001 · contractual data-processing agreements |
| Phishing impersonation | Medium | Medium | DMARC=quarantine min · `List-Unsubscribe` headers · DKIM/SPF verified |
| Push notification spoofing | Low | Low | VAPID-signed payloads · service worker validates origin |
| Refresh token theft | Low | High | HTTP-only Secure SameSite cookie · path-scoped `/api/auth/refresh` · reuse-detection family revoke |
| Telegram channel hijack | Low | Medium | Daily-rotated link token · per-chat ID binding (FR-NOTIF-003) |
| Honey-style commission scandal | Low | Catastrophic | Plan §A3 five-principles firewall · disclosure on every surface · open-source revenue calculator |
| Government data request | Low | Medium | Documented response protocol · only respond to court orders · publish transparency report counts |

## 5. Technical & Organizational Measures (TOM)

### 5.1 Technical

- TLS 1.2+ everywhere (Vercel + Railway + Atlas all enforce).
- AES-256 encryption-at-rest (Atlas + Neon defaults).
- Secret management via Doppler; no `.env` committed.
- Auth.js v5 pinned (FR-AUTH-001); JWT refresh rotation (FR-AUTH-003).
- Rate limits on every endpoint (FR-AUTH-001 §1 #12, FR-WORKER-002, FR-NOTIF-001 §1 #10).
- PII redaction in PostHog: emails hashed `sha256(email + POSTHOG_PII_SALT).slice(0,16)`.
- IP truncation: `/24` IPv4, `/64` IPv6 in analytics.
- Audit chain: BRAIN-style append-only log for every state change.

### 5.2 Organizational

- DPO appointment (this filing).
- Annual TOM review.
- Quarterly Transparency Report (public).
- Breach detection: `breach-detector.ts` pages founder via Slack within 30s.
- 72-hour A05 notification process (template in `A05-breach-notification-template.md`).

### 5.3 Procedural

- Privacy-by-design: every new FR requires `risk_if_skipped` field + data-flow review.
- Consent versioning: `users.consents` stores `policyVersion`; re-consent on Policy update.
- Right-to-erasure: `POST /v1/me/delete-account` soft-tombstone immediate, hard-purge within 72h.
- Right-to-portability: `POST /v1/me/data-export` returns ZIP within 30 days.
