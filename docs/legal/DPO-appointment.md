## 2. Statutory eligibility (Art. 28)

Per Article 28(3), an organization with fewer than 50 employees may designate the founder/CEO as DPO provided no conflict of interest exists.

CyberSkill JSC currently has 10 employees (including 2 interns) and 1 senior tech lead. Founder is the natural DPO candidate.

### 2.1 Conflict-of-interest declaration

The appointee:
- Does NOT directly execute personal-data processing operations (delegation to Senior Tech Lead for engineering matters).
- Will recuse from any decision where personal financial interest conflicts with data subject interest (e.g., trading off privacy vs. revenue).
- Reports directly to the Board of Directors (not to engineering management).
- Will retain independent counsel access (Tilleke & Gibbins or equivalent) for any decision where in-house judgment may be biased.

## 3. Statutory responsibilities (Art. 28)

The DPO is the responsible party for:

1. Filing the DPIA (`DPIA-2026-05.md`) with A05 within 60 days of start of processing.
2. Maintaining the Record of Processing Activities (RoPA) — embedded in the DPIA §2 above and updated quarterly.
3. Responding to data subject requests (DSR) — access (Art. 14), correction (Art. 15), erasure (Art. 16), restriction (Art. 19), portability (Art. 18), objection (Art. 20) — within statutory timelines.
4. Notifying A05 within 72 hours of any breach (template at `A05-breach-notification-template.md`).
5. Quarterly Transparency Report publication (`/transparency/<YYYY-Q[1-4]>`).
6. Annual re-review of TOMs and the DPIA.
7. Internal training on PDPL compliance (founder + interns + senior lead each year).
8. Liaison with A05 on audits or inquiries.

## 4. Resources granted to the DPO

To carry out the above, the appointee has unrestricted access to:

- All systems holding personal data (Atlas, Neon, Upstash, Resend, PostHog, Sentry, Stripe, VNPay, MoMo, Telegram).
- Doppler workspace secrets (for incident response).
- Slack `#founder-incidents` channel (breach alerts).
- Founder time budget: ≥ 4 hours/week on average, ≥ 40 hours during incident response or quarterly cycles.
- Counsel budget: ≥ 30M ₫ one-shot pre-launch, ≥ 10M ₫/quarter retainer (TBD).

## 5. Backup contact (escalation when DPO unavailable)

Backup contact named for after-hours / vacation periods:

- **Primary backup:** Senior Tech Lead (to be hired by 2026-06 per plan §G3).
- **Interim (until backup hire):** Founder remains on-call; breach-detector continues to page Slack `#founder-incidents` to founder's mobile.
- **Escalation if both unreachable for > 6 hours during a breach:** outside counsel (Tilleke & Gibbins or equivalent) instructed to act as interim DPO for filing only.

## 6. Termination & succession

The appointment ends on any of:

- Founder departure from CyberSkill JSC.
- Headcount > 50 (statutory threshold change; full external DPO required).
- Board-passed resolution.
- Mutual agreement.

On termination, a new DPO MUST be appointed within 30 days; appointment letter is re-filed with A05.
