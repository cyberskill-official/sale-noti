# SaleNoti Affiliate Ethics Principles

1. **Transparency:** disclosure appears on every surface where affiliate is involved.
2. **User initiated:** affiliate links activate only on explicit user click; no auto-redirects.
3. **Coupon respect:** no auto-application; known codes are copy-paste only.
4. **Cookie respect:** do not override affiliate cookies from other publishers or KOCs.
5. **No hiding better deals:** ranking uses observable user-value signals, never internal commission rate.

Quarterly review owner: Founder/DPO plus counsel.
