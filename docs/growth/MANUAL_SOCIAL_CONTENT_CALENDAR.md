# SaleNoti Manual Social Content And Posting Schedule

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-05-18  
**Scope:** FR-GROW-002 share-deal flow, FR-GROW-003 Mega Sale Mode, Telegram launch support.

This document exists because social auto-posting must stay manually gated for the first sale events. The product can prepare content, links, and timing, but a human should review and post until policy, copy tone, and affiliate disclosure are proven safe.

---

## 1. Posting Principles

- Always disclose affiliate relationship in the post or first comment.
- Never promise a price is the lowest ever unless SaleNoti price history confirms it.
- Never imply Shopee endorses SaleNoti.
- Do not post hidden affiliate links. Use the SaleNoti share/deal page or disclosed short link.
- Prefer Vietnamese copy first; English only for investor/build-in-public channels.
- Keep Telegram high-signal: fewer alerts, clearer prices, no spam bursts.

Disclosure line:

> SaleNoti có thể nhận hoa hồng tiếp thị liên kết nếu bạn mua qua liên kết. Giá bạn trả không đổi.

## 5. Manual Posting Checklist

Before posting:

- Link opens and renders on mobile.
- OpenGraph title/image/description preview is correct.
- Affiliate disclosure is visible in post, page, or first comment.
- Price claim is supported by SaleNoti history.
- No raw user identifiers or private watchlist details appear.
- Group rules allow affiliate/deal links.
- UTM/source tag matches channel.

After posting:

- Save post URL in growth tracker.
- Record channel, timestamp, creative, CTA, and UTM.
- Check comments for policy/trust concerns within 2 hours.
- Pause repeated posting if negative feedback appears.
